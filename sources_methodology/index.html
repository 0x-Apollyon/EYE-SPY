Our sources and how we roll !

We monitor multiple darkweb forums, telegram groups, discord groups and signal chats for intel on hacker activity

Due to privacy concerns and to stop the threat actors and hackers from discovering that they are being monitored, we can not reveal all our exact sources but some are as follows


Breachforums : BreachForums is an English-language black hat–hacking crime forum. The website acted as an alternative and successor to RaidForums

Leakbase : Indexes and has sold access to billions of usernames and passwords stolen in some of the world largest data breaches

XSS.IS : With a history stretching back to 2013, emerges not only as one of the oldest forums but also as a prominent hub for Russian speaking hackers

Nulled : Hosts a variety of illicit content, including leaked data, compromised identities, credit card information, and tools for illegal activities.

Dread : “Reddit of the dark web” due to its similar interface. Hosts data about drug sales and illegal cyber activity.

Doxbin: One of the dark web's most malicious and notorious sites, publishing personal details regardless of the consequences

Chan Boards: 2Chan, 4Chan and 8Chan are messages boards with little to no moderation and as a result data leaks are often spread there
